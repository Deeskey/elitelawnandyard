About Us

Thank you for visiting! My name is Johnny Martinez, and landscaping and yard work are more than just a job—they're my passion. As the proud owner and operator of this small, family-owned business, I take great pride in serving my community. I’m a Vietnam War veteran, having served in the Marines, and after a fulfilling career with the US Postal Service as both a mail carrier and postmaster, I retired in 2004. Having lived in Utah County for over 60 years, I've gained extensive experience in lawn care, gardening, and a wide range of landscaping projects. At Elite Lawn and Yard, we offer lawn maintenance, fertilization, aeration, and various landscaping services—all at a fair and competitive price. It would be my honor to work with you and help enhance your outdoor space!
